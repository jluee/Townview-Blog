7 Things To Do For Date Night in Tampa!

By Townview Editorial Team

The city of Tampa has more than plenty to offer when it comes to choosing a good spot for a first date. Our team of bloggers decided to put together a list of their favorite spots in Tampa for a date night with your significant other!

7. The Riverwalk

The Riverwalk runs the Hillsborough River in Tampa, locals and tourists can view public art and access any number of restaurants, stores, parks and bike rental shops along the route. Visit the website (www.thetampariverwalk.com) for more information.

6. Tampa Museum of Art

Celebrating its 100th anniversary, the Tampa Museum of Art is a perfect venue to learn about the artistic history of the Tampa Bay region and others around the world. The Tampa Bay museum of Art provides engaging exhibitions and innovative educational programs that emphasize ancient, modern and contemporary art. Visit the website for more information. (www.tampamuseum.org)

5. The Florida Aquarium

A beautiful blue backyard right in Tampa, come see incredible marine life native to Florida and other exotic sea mammals from other regions. A must see attraction in Tampa and great opportunity to learn more about aquatic life and enjoy a weekend date! Visit the website for more information (https://www.flaquarium.org)

4. Empower Adventures

Right in the heart of Tampa, Empower Adventures is the perfect spot to do outdoor challenge courses that include zip lining, paddle board adventures and canopy moonlight tours. Visit the website to book your reservation today. (https://www.zipontampabay.com)

3. Ybor City

A National Historic Landmark right here in Tampa filled with culture and a diverse selection of retail stores, restaurants and bars. Ybor City has a colorful vibe that is brought to life with art, history and enticing food choices. Visit the website for more information (https://yborcityonline.com)
