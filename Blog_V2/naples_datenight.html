7 Date Night Ideas in Naples and Fort Myers!

By Townview Editorial Team

The cities of Fort Myers and Naples have more than plenty to offer when it comes to selecting a good spot for date night. Our team of bloggers decided to put together a list of their favorite places around Fort Myers & Naples.

7. Calusa Nature & Planetarium

Featuring a museum, nature trails and an aviary there are plenty of educational things to do. The Calusa Nature & Planetarium offers live demonstrations and daily programs for all ages to enjoy. Visit their website for more information.

6. Naples Botanical Garden

Home to more than 1,000 different plants, flowers and trees. A perfect place to enjoy a self guided tour while basking in the ambience of nature. Admission is $20 for adults and now open under safety protocols and social distancing standards. If you are looking for tranquility surrounded by unique nature, Naples Botanical Garden is the perfect place. Visit their website for more information.

5. Naples Beach

Stretching more than 1,000 feet out into the Gulf of Mexico, Naples is the perfect place for a casual walk on a date night. According to annual tourists, it’s the absolute best spot to take in a memorizing Florida sunset. Visit  their website for more information.

4. Bell Tower

A premier dining and fashion district in Fort Myers, Bell Tower shops offers many different options for a date night. Bell Tower offers a movie theater, a Dave & Busters, exquisite restaurants and bars that have happy hours and deals for first time guest. Visit their website for more information.

3. Fort Myers River District

Located in Downtown Fort Myers, the River District is a historic landmark in Fort Myers and represents many different businesses. Including several lounges, bars and family owned restaurants that display the true beauty of Fort Myers. Visit their website for more information.

2. 5th Avenue South

Located in Downtown Naples, 5th Avenue South offers upscale dining, shopping, antique shops and live entertainment weekly. Home to exquisite art galleries, high end boutiques and an assortment of restaurants, you can find a little of everything you need while in the area. Visit their website for more information.

1. Fort Myers Beach

The main attraction in Fort Myers! The beach is filled with lots of boutiques, family owned restaurants and live entertainment. Jet ski rentals are available on the beach to residents and tourists. Times Square is a hidden gem in Fort Myers that includes many retail boutiques and authentic restaurants. Visit their website for more information.
